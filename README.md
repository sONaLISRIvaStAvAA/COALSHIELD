# 🛡️⛏️ CoalShield – Smart Safety Jacket for Underground Worker Protection

> *Protecting those who work where danger lives.* 🚨🌍

## 🌟 Overview

**CoalShield** is an **Industrial IoT-based smart safety jacket** built to enhance the protection of coal mine workers and engineers operating in hazardous underground and industrial environments 🏭⛏️

Mining environments can be unpredictable and dangerous, exposing workers to risks like:

⚠️ Low oxygen levels  
🌡️ Extreme temperature conditions  
💧 Humidity fluctuations  
🫀 Worker fatigue or health emergencies  
🚶 Falls or sudden inactivity  
📍 Difficulty in tracking worker location  
⏳ Delayed emergency response  

CoalShield tackles these challenges by combining **wearable sensing technology, embedded systems, live monitoring, and automated emergency alerts** into one smart protective solution 💙⚡

## 🛠️ Hardware Components

### 🧠 Core Processing
- 🍓 **Raspberry Pi 4 Model B+**  
  Central processing unit for sensor interfacing, data handling, and system control.

### 📡 Sensors
- 🎯 **MPU6050**  
  Accelerometer + Gyroscope for motion detection, movement tracking, and fall detection.

- ❤️ **MAX30102**  
  Monitors **Heart Rate** and **Blood Oxygen Saturation (SpO₂)**.

- 🌡️ **DHT11**  
  Measures **Temperature** and **Humidity** levels.

- ⛰️ **BMP180**  
  Monitors **Atmospheric Pressure** and **Altitude-related environmental changes**.

- 📍 **NEO-6M GPS Module**  
  Provides worker location tracking support for emergency assistance and monitoring.

### 🚨 Alert System
- 🔊 **Buzzer**  
  Triggers immediate audible alerts during hazardous conditions.

## ⚙️ Working Principle

CoalShield continuously gathers real-time data from multiple sensors integrated into the smart jacket 🧥📡

### 🫀 Health Monitoring
The **MAX30102** tracks:
- ❤️ Heart Rate
- 🩸 Blood Oxygen Saturation (SpO₂)

This helps detect worker fatigue or potential health emergencies.

### 🌍 Environmental Monitoring
The environment is monitored using:

- 🌡️ **DHT11** → Temperature & Humidity
- ⛰️ **BMP180** → Pressure & Altitude changes

This helps identify unsafe working conditions.

### 🏃 Motion Detection
The **MPU6050** tracks:

- Worker movement
- Orientation changes
- Sudden falls
- Prolonged inactivity

Helping detect accidents or distress situations ⚠️

### 📍 Location Tracking
The **NEO-6M GPS Module** provides location assistance for worker tracking and emergency support.

### 🚨 Emergency Alerts
If abnormal conditions are detected:

⚠️ Unsafe environmental readings  
⚠️ Abnormal health parameters  
⚠️ Sudden fall/inactivity  

The **buzzer instantly activates** to alert nearby personnel 🔊🚨
